Common Questions

Quick answers to the most frequently asked questions about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for website development varies depending on the complexity and requirements of your project. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while more complex sites can take 2-3 months or more.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing is based on the specific requirements of your project. We offer both fixed-price quotes and hourly rates depending on the nature of the work. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Do you provide website maintenance?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. Our maintenance services include regular updates, security monitoring, and technical support.

Can you help with website content?

Yes, we can assist with content creation, including copywriting, image selection, and overall content strategy to ensure your website effectively communicates your message.

Do you offer hosting services?

Yes, we provide reliable hosting solutions for all websites we develop. Our hosting packages include regular backups, security measures, and technical support.

What technologies do you use?

We primarily use Django and Tailwind CSS for our web development projects, which provides a robust, secure, and scalable foundation for websites of all types.
